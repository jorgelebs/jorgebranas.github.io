Frequent questions

Tuscany is a very visited area in Italy and many visitors have questions about which restaurants to choose to eat out.

Which one is closer to the center?

The closest restaurant is the Trattoria Antico Petrolio, but others like Ristorante Retrò and Osteria Cacio Vino Trallallà are relatively close as well.

Which are the best in relation quality-price-zone?

The best, without a doubt, are Ristorante Retrò and Osteria Cacio Vino Trallallà. These have very good food, prices between 30€ and 50€, and are close to downtown. However, Ventuno District is very far from the center.
